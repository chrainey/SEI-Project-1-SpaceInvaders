Move Spaceship

Press the Start Game button to start the wave of enemies. Press the left and right arrows to move your ship.

Destroy Enemies

Use the space bar to fire at the enemies before they reach you at the bottom of the screen. Make sure to avoid the bombs that the enemies drop.

Winning/Losing

If you are hit by a bomb your will lose a life. Once you have lost all 3 lives you have lost the game. If the aliens hit the ground or yourself you will also lose the game. To win you need to destroy all enemies without losing all of your lives before
